# MedBridge - 60-Second Demo Pitch Script

**[0:00 - 0:10] The Problem Statement**
*(On screen: Presenter looking at the camera confidently)*
"In Nigeria and across Africa, when people feel sick, they often panic or resort to self-medication because seeing a doctor immediately is either too expensive or too far away. We need a reliable, calm first point of contact."

**[0:10 - 0:40] The Live Demo Flow**
*(On screen: Screencast of MedBridge on mobile layout. Click "Check My Symptoms")*
"Meet MedBridge. It's an AI-powered health triage assistant designed with modern African warmth and medical trust. Instead of googling symptoms and getting terrified, you just describe how you feel. 
*(Click the microphone button and speak a symptom in Pidgin/English)*
Watch this: I can just speak my symptoms naturally, even in Pidgin English or Yoruba. 
*(Click Analyze. Show the animated typing dots, then the Response Card appears)*
Instantly, MedBridge analyzes the symptoms. It gives a clear Urgency Level, a simple explanation without medical jargon, and actionable home care tips. 
*(Scroll down to show the buttons)*
If it's an emergency, our Geolocation Hospital Finder immediately connects you to the nearest clinic on Google Maps. I can also instantly share this structured report with a family member or my doctor via WhatsApp with one click."

**[0:40 - 0:60] The Vision & Impact**
*(On screen: Show the Follow-Up chat feature, then cut back to Presenter)*
"MedBridge even lets you ask follow-up questions, just like texting a doctor. Best of all? It's completely free, runs on the Gemini 1.5 Flash API, and requires zero login, ensuring total privacy. MedBridge isn't replacing doctors; it's bridging the gap between uncertainty and professional care for millions of Nigerians."
